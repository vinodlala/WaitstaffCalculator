Welcome to Waitstaff Calculator

This app helps wait staff at restaurants compute meal costs and track their tips of the course of a shift.

Click on New Meal, then enter the Base Meal Price, Tax Percent and Tip Percent. Click on Submit to compute the meal cast and track the tip, or click Cancel to start over.

Click on My Earnings to see the Tip Total, Meal Count, Average Tip per Meal and Average Tip Percentage per Meal.
